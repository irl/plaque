Commemorative Plaques Record Details

Hardgate Well

Plaque Transcript: This spring figured in the Battle of Justice Mills fought on 13th September 1644 between the Royalist forces under the Marquis of Montrose and the citizens of Aberdeen. George MCI. Keith City Architect Rupert S. Lennox Lord Provost Erected by the Corporation of the City of Aberdeen. 1970

Location : Hardgate

Area : Central Aberdeen

Plaque Type :Non-standard / Memorial

About Hardgate Well :This commemorates the site of this historic well which featured in the battle of Justice Mills, fought on 13 September 1644 between the Royalist forces of Montrose and the citizens of Aberdeen
