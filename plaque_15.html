Commemorative Plaques Record Details

Ian Fleming (1906 - 1994)

Location : 15 Fonthill Road

Area : South Aberdeen

Plaque Type :Yellow

About Ian Fleming :Artist. He is best known as an engraver and etcher but he also played a fundamental part in the visual arts in Aberdeen. His career spanned almost an entire century as a painter, printmaker and teacher at Glasgow School of Art and later at Gray's School of Art in Aberdeen where he was Head of School.
