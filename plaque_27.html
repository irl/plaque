Commemorative Plaques Record Details

Robert Laws (1851 - 1934)

Location : 39 Thistle Street

Area : Central Aberdeen

Plaque Type :Yellow

About Robert Laws :Pioneer of African Education. Born in Aberdeen he graduated from Aberdeen University and Edinburgh United Presbyterian Theological College. He was ordained a medical missionary in the Livingstonia Mission and founded the Hope Waddel Institute at Calabar, West Africa.
