Commemorative Plaques Record Details

Exchequer Row

Location : Exchequer Row

Area : Central Aberdeen

Plaque Type :Court

About Exchequer Row :This commemorates the site of the Royal Exchequer and possibly Aberdeen’s mint, which functioned intermittently in this area from the reign of David I (1124-1153) to James III (1460-1488). Coins issued here bore the name of the mint ‘Villa Aberdon’.
