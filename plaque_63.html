Commemorative Plaques Record Details

Sir Alexander Anderson (1802 - 1887)

Location : North Pier

Area : North Aberdeen

Plaque Type :Non-standard / Memorial

About Sir Alexander Anderson :Financier and City Planner. Born in Strichen and educated at Aberdeen Grammar School and Marischal College he launched commercial designs for the North of Scotland Insurance Company and the North of Scotland Bank in 1837. He is best known for his ‘parentage’ of the Great North of Scotland Railway Company and was elected Lord Provost of Aberdeen in 1859. PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS PLAQUE IS NOT ACCESSIBLE TO THE PUBLIC
